Stuff I Want

So, recently, with my birthday coming up and all (May 26), people have been asking me what I want. My general response is someone along the lines of “Well, gee, I don’t know….” So, I’m putting this list up here so I can just point people at it and I can just add and remove things as I go.

Guidelines

Now, you don’t have to get me something that’s on the list. If you’re going to stray, here are a few general guidelines.

  1. If it’s not useful, I won’t like it. I try to keep my possessions fairly minimal, so this is important.
  2. Unless you’re someone I converse with regularly about computers or music, don’t even think about getting me something related to either of those areas.
  3. If you don’t know what to get me, the list is going to help you more than the clerk at the local gift shop.

The List

These are all listed in order of my desire. Don’t feel the need to just pick the first one in your price range, though – if there’s something on the list that seems attuned to our particular relationship or that makes you think “I really enjoyed this, I should share that experience with Greg”, I’ll appreciate the relevance and be more likely to remember who got it for me.
