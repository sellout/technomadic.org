Where can this FAQ be found?
The Wearable Computing FAQ is posted to comp.sys.wearables periodically. It can also be found on the Web at http://technomadic.org/info/faqs/wearcomp.shtml. There is also at least one mirror. It's located at http://www.wearcomp.org/wearcompfaq.html.
Who is responsible for this FAQ?
This FAQ began as a compendium of FAQs. The component parts are still available in their original forms. The MIT Wearable Computing FAQ (http://lcs.www.media.mit.edu/projects/wearables/FAQ/FAQ.txt) -- referenced by [MIT] -- is maintained by Josh Weaver <weaverj@mit.edu>. The Wearcam FAQ (http://wearcomp.org/faq.txt) -- referenced by [Mann] -- is maintained by Steve Mann <mann@eecg.toronto.edu>. Gregory Martin Pfeil <pfeilgm@technomadic.org> is the current maintainer of this FAQ.
How can I add my input?
Send an e-mail to faqs@technomadic.org with the subject "wearcomp". If you don't wish to have your name or e-mail address added to our contributor list, please say so.
