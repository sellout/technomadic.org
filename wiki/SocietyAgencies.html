Society Agencies

Diplomacy

Act as a mediator between us and other states.

Language

Maintain dictionary and entymology in all its forms, and provide new terms as necessary. (SocietyLanguage)

Military

Train citizens in the use of weapons and develop strategy and tactics for use on our home terrain. (SocietyMilitary)

Monitoring

Watch government officials to make sure that openness is maintained without sacrificing their privacy.

Openness

Maintain the openness of the government, and promote openness in

other areas.

Police

To enforce the regulations provided by other agencies.

Privacy

Maintain the privacy of each citizen, and promote new methods of

securing it.

Safety

Create regulations and recommendations pertaining to public safety.

Science

Monitor scientific developments and disburse funding.

Technology

Decide which technologies should be adopted by the state and

promote development. (SocietyTechnology)

Transit

Maintain the transit infrastructure and manage public

transportation. (SocietyTransit)
