Society Arms

The right to bear arms is often a touchy subject. However, following our premise that no object is wrong in and of itself, all arms are legal. The exception to this, is atomic weapons. Keeping with treaties in other nations, atomic weapons will be forbidden in the state, or in the possession of a citizen.

SocietyMilitary has a description of the certification process necessary to be able to possess arms.
